FAQ

  • Home
  • Frequently asked questions

1. What to do if I have still not received the order?

Contact details of the seller have been provided with every purchase, you can directly get in touch with them to enquire about the order.

2. What are the postal rates?

Since most purchases are in-house (within the college), we do not feel the necessity of including a postal service.

3. Do you deliver?

Since most purchases are in-house (within the college), we do not feel the necessity of a delivery system. Orders can be placed on the website, and the books can be passed over by hand after fixing up the details with the seller.

4. What if the book is not in good condition?

In such a scenario, it is upto the buyer to decide whether or not to go ahead with the purchase. It is most likely that such a situation will not arise, since this platform ensures quality services.

5. Is there a rental facility?

Since the primary purpose of the platform is to enable sale of old books and equipment, there is no rental facility.

6. Is this facility available inter-college?

Currently, it's only intra-college. We will be soon implementing it within different colleges who are ready to collaborate.
