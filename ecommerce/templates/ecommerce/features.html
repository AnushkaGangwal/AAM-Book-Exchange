Books and Equipment

Old books and equipment related to engineering drawing can be sold with the help of this system. The seller can upload his products, and a buyer can browse through the list of available products to choose his required books.

Departments

Computers, IT, EXTC, Mechanical etc.

Services

Textbooks, reference books, ED equipment etc.

Subjects

Data Structures, ED, Thermodynamics, Applied Maths etc.

Books and Equipment Description

A comprehensive list of textbooks and reference books uploaded by various sellers has been provided. Buyers can browse for their requirements, or have a look at the suggestions provided considering their year and departmment. An order can then be placed with the seller. In case of multiple buyers, first come first serve policy will be used. The buyer details will be sent to the seller. They can get in touch with each other and fix up the details of the purchase.
